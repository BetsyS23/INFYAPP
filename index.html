🌟 KING

"you can rule over any job app"

🎯 Pitch

Introducing KING, an app design to help people get ready for work by providing support for things like missing IDs, resumes, and understanding job requirements. King gives simple step-by-step guides connects users to local resoruces, and provides direct assistance to help people of all ages become eligiblefor jobs. Our goal is to make sure anyone who needs help with getting ready for work can find the support the need.

🌍 Theme Implementation

While Outplay Hunger focuses on providing individuals with access to food security, KING connects directly to this theme by addressing the crucial need for stable employment-helping people secure jobs that can lead to financial independence and the ability to access essential resoruces like food. By helping individuals become ready for the workforce, KING works hand-in-hand with initilatives like Outplau Hunger, this helps people face and overcome the different challenges that come with money and job problems.

🧠 Why We Built This

We built this app because many people struggle to find jobs not because they don't want to work, but because they're missing important things like an ID, a resume, or other job requirements. We saw a need for a tool that could guide people step by step, connect them with local help, and make the job search easier. Our goal is to support anyone, at any age, in getting ready for work and building a better future.
